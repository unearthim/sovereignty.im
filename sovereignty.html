Sovereignty is the right to rule. In the digital age, it is the right to rule oneself.

It is the foundational principle that an individual's identity, data, and assets belong to them and them alone. It is a declaration of independence from the centralized platforms that have acted as custodians for too long.

This is the landmark for the entire self-sovereign identity movement. It is for the protocol, the foundation, or the alliance that is writing the constitution for a user-controlled internet.

For the self-governed individual.
